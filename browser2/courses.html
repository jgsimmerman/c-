Why Cosmetology 

 

 

 

Cosmetology
You bleed haircolor. There was a line of friends waiting for do's out your bedroom door on prom day. If OPI came out with a new nail color, you knew about it first and had it on release day. You look at people in the mall and know just what hair style would really improve their outlook on life.

Cosmetology is the fashion career for you! In just 1500 hours, under the guidance of our multi-talented instructors, you can learn what you need to succeed in the world of beauty. Cosmetology includes every aspect of haircare, skincare, and nails (including body waxing and artificial nails). We'll even include Spa classes absolutely free! So call us today to get started. Just think, in one year.....


 

Junior Room in Battle Creek

Manicuring/Nail Technology
Ahhhhh, a relaxing pedicure on a stressful day. A quick manicure to lift your spirits. Beautiful nail enhancements of acrylic, gel or fiberglass. Dazzling nail art. Our nail techs learn all this and more! Learn in-demand skills like Hot Stone Pedicures, Spa manicures, and Aromatherapy.

Portage offers a 400 clock hour program  (that's just 12 weeks!), and Battle Creek offers a 600* clock hour program. Imagine a fabulous new career in 3-6 months! Call us today to set up an appointment to plan your future.

*The 600 hour program is PELL eligible. Financial aid is available for those who qualify.

 

Manicure & Pedicure area at the Battle Creek campus
Instructor Training
Do you enjoy helping people learn new ideas and techniques? Do you have a current Michigan Cosmetology or Nail Tech/Manicuring license? If so, you may qualify for training to become a licensed instructor. As an instructor, you help train the next generation of cosmetologists and nail techs. The hours for Instructor Trainees are flexible: we'll work around your salon schedule!  Contact us today to schedule an appointment to discuss your instructor training!
