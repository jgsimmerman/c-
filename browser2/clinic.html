Client Services 

 

Wright Beauty Academy offers a full-service clinic to the public, using Matrix color at both schools. Clients enjoy a complete salon experience, from top to toe.  From our haircuts that range from $5 to $7, including shampoo, to spa pedicures, we offer salon services at a fraction of salon prices. Our students are trained in salon techniques and running a professional salon, our clients receive professional services at great prices, and everyone wins! All work is performed by supervised senior students, and our clinics are overseen by licensed, experienced instructors.


We encourage appointments, and strive to accommodate requested students. However, due to graduations, absences, and other factors,  we cannot guarantee your appointment will be with the student requested. Walk-ins are welcome; however, because students are sometimes not as fast, please understand if we don't have time to accommodate your service at that time. Both schools close at 4:30, and last appointments are between 1:00 & 3:30, depending on the service.
