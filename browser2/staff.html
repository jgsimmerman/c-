 Jessica Gieske - Owner / Instructor
Jessica is the 3rd generation owner/instructor of Wright Beauty Academy. Her grandmother, Selma Wright, opened her dream school in 1944, her dad, Robert Burrell Sr., took it over in 1969, and Jes took the helm in 1979. She literally grew up in the business. With a Bachelor's degree in Marketing from Western Michigan University, Jes helps senior students and graduates find unique ways to market their strengths and skills to further their careers. Jes is a licensed Cosmetology instructor, and a past Vice-President of the Michigan Cosmetology Schools Association. She has attended 4 NACCAS Reaccreditation  workshops, covering over 20 years of accreditation. She completed over 600 hours of formal massage training, and  today she and her husband own 2 cosmetology schools, and a salon/spa.

 Victor Gieske - Owner / Instructor
Victor attended KCC & LCC as a business management major. He has been a financial aid administrator for 18 years. He has attended 4 NACCAS Reaccreditation workshops, and was active in Financial Aid seminars. He is active in raising money for Breast Cancer awareness, did a roof sit for Abused Children of Michigan, raised $2000 for West Michigan Cancer Association, and rides in the Harley Toys for Tots run each year.

Cheryl Simmerman - Director / Instructor
Cheryl has been with Wright Beauty Academy since 1995. She started as one of the first licensed nail instructors in Michigan. She sat on the board that developed the state board test, was published in Nails & Nail Pro magazines, and developed the current 600 hour nail curriculum at the Battle Creek campus.  She was one of the first licensed Estheticians, and the first licensed Esthetician Instructor in the state. Finally taking the plunge, she received her Cosmetology & Cosmetology Instructor licenses in 2000. Cheryl belongs to NCA, and has taken extensive advanced training, including advanced razor cutting with Nick Arrojo, a hair stylist on TLC's What Not To Wear.
